Cleveland Indians Highlights

  • The Cleveland Indians are affectionately referred to as "The Tribe".
  • The Indians finished the 2015 season with a record of 81 wins and 80 losses and a race for a wildcard position in the playoffs.
  • The Indians play at Progressive Field, which was previously known as Jacobs Field.
  • One of the Indians' best ball players was Sandy Alomar Jr., who served as first base coach last season.
  • The Cleveland Indians have not won a World Series Championship since 1948 .
  • The Indians made it the World Series in the 2016 series, but lost game 7 in extra innings 4-3 on November 2, 2016
