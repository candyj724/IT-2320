Cleveland Browns Highlights

  • In 2015, the Cleveland Browns finished with an overall record of 7 wins and 9 losses in the regular season.
  • The Cleveland Browns are coached by Coach Hue Jackson.
  • The Browns were previously owned by Cleveland's most hated man, Art Modell.
  • The Browns have had several quarterback controversies over the years, this year involved Josh McCown and Johnny Manziel.
  • The Browns'finished with their worst record ever of 1 win and 15 losses.
