Cleveland Cavaliers Highlights

  • Last year, the Cleveland Cavaliers finished with an overall record of 53 wins and 20 losses in the regular season.
  • Lebron James returned to the Cavaliers after leaving for four years to play with the Miami Heat, and winning three NBA Championship rings.
  • The Cavaliers made it to the NBA Finals last season, and made a spectacular run for the championship despite numerous injuries.
  • Some of our more notable players this year are Mo Williams, JR Smith, Tristan Thompson, Matthew Delladova, Kyrie Irving, Kevin Love, and Iman Schmpert.
  • The city of Cleveland is "ALL IN" as the Cleveland Cavaliers seek to gain the city's 1st NBA Championship in the 2015-2016 season.
  • Last year, the Cavaliers overcame a 3-1 game deficit against the Golden State Warriors to become the NBA Champions.
  • In celebration of Cleveland's first NBA championship, millions of fans flooded downtown Cleveland to witness the parade in the teams honor.
