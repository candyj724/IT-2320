Repping my City: Cleveland's Finest

Cleveland is a city that is fortunate to have several sports teams.
We are sports fanatics here in Cleveland, and we support our teams, win or lose.
We have our courageous Cleveland Cavaliers, our barking and beloved Cleveland Browns, and are ever so exciting Cleveland Indians to name a few teams.
I was born and raised in Cleveland, and I will be a diehard fan of any Cleveland team till the day I die
